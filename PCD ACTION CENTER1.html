PROJECT BRIEFERS

Under the leadership of Governor Arthur Defensor Jr., the Province of Iloilo has aggressively pursued its MoRProGRes (Movement for a Robust, Progressive, Globally Competitive, and Resilient Province of Iloilo) agenda, backed by a historic ₱6.88 billion executive budget for 2026. Recent executive actions and project briefers highlight a massive push for localized infrastructure and social services, including the turnover of multi-million peso water systems in Maasin, solar power projects in Tigbauan and Alimodian, and significant school building constructions funded through the Special Education Fund. A central pillar of this update is the launch of the 4K for MoRProGRes program (Kapayapaan, Kaayusan at Katatagan Tungo sa Kaunlaran), which integrates peace and order with climate resilience. Additionally, as of February 2026, the province and city are in the spotlight for hosting high-level international events, such as the 49th ASEAN Economic Integration meeting, prompting executive orders to ensure public safety and streamlined security protocols.

Welcome to the TALAMDAN Geospatial Portal!

TALAMDAN stands for the Technologically Advanced Lens for the Assessment and Management of Development and Actions Needed. It is a user-friendly geospatial database and Geographic Information System (GIS) hub. managed by the Provincial Planning and Development Office (PPDO).

Access to this website is restricted to the Iloilo Provincial Capitol local network; please visit https://191.168.54.133/offices.html while connected to the Capitol's internal Wi-Fi or LAN.

IPGFMS

What is IPGFMS?

As the centralized digital backbone of the Provincial Capitol, the IPGFMS serves as a comprehensive data repository connecting all IPG offices. Moving beyond the scope of standard accounting software, it is designed as a 'living archive' that documents the province’s administrative life and history.

ABOUT US

WHAT IS PCD ACTION CENTER?

Pursuant to Executive Order No. 111, Series of 2025, the Provincial Community Defense Action for MoRProGRes Iloilo. "Kapayapaan, Kaayusan at Katatagan Tungo sa Kaunlaran ng Bagong Pilipinas" or 4K for MoRProGRes, a system for an intensified, integrated and collaborative community action for peace, order and resilience towards National Development in the defense of the general welfare of the citizens, and the LGUs against internal and external threats.

To stay informed and prepared, you can also visit our 4K for MoRProGRes Dashboard for the latest updates.

PROJECT REGISTRY - OVERVIEW

MoRProGRes Iloilo: A Strategic Vision for Provincial Infrastructure. Infrastructure in the Province of Iloilo is currently undergoing a transformative phase, driven by the MoRProGRes Iloilo (Movement for a Resilient, Progressive, Responsive, and Global Iloilo) development strategy. The primary purpose of these projects is to uplift the lives of Ilonggos by addressing long-standing challenges in food security, disaster resilience, and economic connectivity.
